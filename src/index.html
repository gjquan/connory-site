The Story

The Beginning

When Greg spotted Connie across a crowded dating app, he immediately had heart eyes. On their first date, he wooed her with sushi and took her to the Lawrence Hall of Science in Berkeley to enjoy the amazing views. Greg was super nervous and quiet because he couldn't believe he was lucky enough to be on a date with such a sweet, down to earth, beautiful, and amazing woman. Connie thought Greg was cute, thoughtful, and sweet, and had the potential to be her foodie partner in crime.

Connie and Greg's love blossomed as they discovered their common interests: being dorky, cute stuff, sweet gestures, inside jokes and phrases, food, boba, old school hip hop and R&B, games, and the Golden State Warriors.

After dating for a while, our lovebirds moved into an apartment together and bought a house not long after. Greg knew he wanted to marry Connie, but he wanted to make sure the proposal was memorable and meaningful. After a few weeks of ring shopping, he had an idea of the ring style that she liked. He started to formulate a plan...

The Proposal

After their first day ring shopping, Connie had picked out a preliminary favorite, which happened to be the first engagement ring she tried on. The following weekend, Connie was travelng for work, so Greg sneakily ordered the ring while she was away and started to brainstorm proposal ideas. He wanted to involve their closest friends and family in the proposal and celebration. He enlisted the help of Connie's close friends Stef, Rox, and Ashley, as well as Greg's close friends Norvelle, Estella, and Jeremiah, to create a special day for Connie.

The Connory Story

The Proposal

Connie thinks she is going out to a casual lunch with her best friends, but her day turns into a scavenger hunt adventure guided by Greg and Connie's close friends. She ends up visting seven special locations, finding a friend at each location to give her clues about the next destination along with some photos and mementos of Greg and Connie. The first clue is a scrapbook that Connie can add things to as she goes from place to place. The final clue will lead Connie to Greg, who will be anxiously waiting to pop the question.

Chapter 1

Location
Shimizu Sushi, Oakland
Significance
Connie loves sushi! (and Greg too)
This is the restaurant where they had their first date.

Stef and Rox suggest lunch at Shimizu Sushi, which "coincidentally" happens to be the restaurant where Greg and Connie has their first date. Estella "coincidentally" runs into Connie during the meal and delivers a package containing the scrapbook, craft supplies, tissues, and a polaroid camera. Inside the scrapbook is the first scavenger hunt clue: a short story about two love birds named PenQuan and FlaminCo. Various words and letters are highlighted in the story, which spell out the location of the next clue. The story also includes a hint about how to unlock the second clue at the next location.

Chapter 2

Location
Warriors Team Store, Walnut Creek
Significance
Greg converted Connie into a huge Warriors fan and they watch all the games together. Connie’s favorite player is Klay Thompson #11; Greg’s is Steph Curry #30. Their anniversary is 11/30.

Stef and Rox take Connie to the Warriors Team Store, since Connie and Greg are huge Warriors fans. Norvelle "coincidentally" runs into Connie while they are shopping and delivers a package containing a mini locked Golden State Warriors gym locker. Using the clues from the short story, Connie figures out that the combination to the locker is their anniversary: 1130. Inside the locker is a newspaper article about the GSW championship rings and how the players looked horrible in photographs with unmanicured hands. The article includes "quotes" from players (Klay, Steph, KD, and Swaggy P.) about the importance of having nice nails. Also included in the locker is a coupon for a "free" manicure from a nail salon, expiring later that day.

Chapter 3

Location
Mandara Nail Spa, Dublin
Significance
Connie is always fashionable and stylish, and her hands will soon be closely photographed!

Our adventure party travels to the nail salon to redeem their "free" coupon. Ashley "coincidentally" runs into Connie while they are there and asks for help solving the interesting crossword puzzle that she was working on. The puzzle includes clues about Connie and Greg that only they would know. Once the crossword is completed, the clues spell out the words "milk tea with green tea ice cream", which is Connie's favorite drink.

Chapter 4

Location
Cafe Tapioca, Dublin
Significance
Connie and Greg drink a lot of boba. Connie has been going to Cafe Tapioca for years.

The journey continues at Cafe Tapioca, Connie's childhood drink spot. Jeremiah "coincidentally" runs into Connie while she is ordering a drink. While making small talk, Jeremiah mentions that he has a "friend" who is interested in working at the Stance store where Connie's is a manager. He hands her a resume packet for a guy named Greg Quan that lists his extensive skills and experience as a boyfriend. The resume alludes to interesting things in the back room of the store.

Chapter 5

Location
Stance SF Premium, Livermore
Significance
Connie is the manager here and her Stance family is very close-knit.

Connie, Stef, and Rox arrive at the Stance store at the SF Premium outlets in Livermore. They walk in to the sounds of John Legend playing on the store speakers - Connie and Greg recently attended his Darkness and Light concert at the Greek Theatre in Berkeley and it was one of the best concerts they have ever attended. The store staff inform Connie that someone "coincidentally" just dropped off a job application packet. They also mention that there was something weird going on with the lights in back room, so they turned them off. Inside the application packet are some UV light pens and John Legend ticket stubs. Connie goes into the back room with the UV lights and discovers a clue - Greek letters written in invisible ink - along with a photograph of the Greek Theatre in Berkeley with a section missing.

Chapter 6

Location
Greek Theatre, Berkeley
Significance
Connie and Greg enjoy John Legend's music and recently attended one of his concerts here.

Our trio travels to the Greek Theatre and examine the area that was missing from the photograph in the previous clue packet. They look around for clues and Stef and Rox "coincidentally" notice something taped to the Greek Theatre sign and conspicuous envelope under a nearby bench. Inside the envelope is a clue with GPS coordinates - where could they lead to?

Chapter 7

Location
Lawrence Hall of Science, Berkeley
Significance
Connie and Greg went here on their first date to take in the amazing views.

Greg and the clue givers met at the Lawrence Hall of Science to prepare to greet Connie. Greg waited at a scenic overlook while Ashley, Brandon, Jeremiah, and Angela stood along the path to Greg holding flowers to give to Connie. Estella and Norvelle escorted Connie down the path while playing their ukeleles. As they reach the end of the path, Greg "coincidentally" runs into Connie and asks for her hand in marriage :)
