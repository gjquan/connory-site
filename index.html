Directions

To the Ceremony

From Highway 13 southbound

  1. Take the Joaquin Miller Road / Lincoln Avenue exit.
  2. Turn left onto Monterey Boulevard.
  3. Turn left onto Joaquin Miller Road.
  4. Follow the road up the hill to Skyline Boulevard and turn left.
  5. Roberts is a mile up Skyline Boulevard on the right.

From Highway 13 northbound

  1. Take the Joaquin Miller Road exit.
  2. Turn right onto Joaquin Miller Road.
  3. Follow the road up the hill to Skyline Boulevard and turn left.
  4. Roberts is a mile up Skyline Boulevard on the right.

Ceremony Parking

Parking is available onsite. Tell the parking attendant you are a guest of the Quan-Co wedding to receive complimentary parking. In the unusual event that the parking lot becomes full, there are some additional spaces a few hundred yards beyond the park on Skyline Boulevard. We encourage carpooling as there are a limited number of spots near the Brazilian Room.

To the Reception

From Roberts Park

  1. Turn left onto Skyline Boulevard.
  2. Turn right at Joaquin Miller Road.
  3. Follow the road down the hill to the stop sign.
  4. After the stop sign, take the ramp on the right onto highway 13 northbound.
  5. Take exit 5C to merge onto highway 24 eastbound.
  6. Take the right tunnel and stay in the right lane.
  7. Immediately following the tunnel, take exit 7A toward Fish Ranch Road.
  8. Fish Ranch Road turns right and continues over the highway.
  9. Turn right onto Grizzly Peak Boulevard.
  10. Turn right onto South Park Drive.
  11. Turn left onto Wildcat Canyon Road.
  12. At the fork, keep right and look for the Brazilian Room parking lot on the right.

Reception Parking

Free parking available onsite. We encourage carpooling as there are a limited number of spots near the Brazilian Room.
